Welcome To JamboPay

Secure Online Payment Getway

JamboPay is an online payment gateway that allows users to securely make and receive payments through mobile phone over internet.Through JamboPay,shoppers can pay for goods and services online while sellers can receive payments for purchases made online.It enables users to pay for bills, fees,make donations and other payments over the internet or on mobile phone. JamboPay allows for mobile payments such as M-PESA, Airtel Money, and T-CASH, Bank payments, Visa and MasterCard Debit and Credit cards.

Corporate Social Responsibility

JamboPay Foundations looks to spread its CRS activities to other regions in the country.
